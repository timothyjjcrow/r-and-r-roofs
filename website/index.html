Roof Replacement in Martinez, CA

From flooded Tar & Gravel to Sleek TPO: a Half-Duplex Rescue in Martinez

We transformed a failing gravel roof—so waterlogged it became a small lake—into a durable, watertight TPO system. Tackling only half of the duplex while ensuring seamless drainage and structural integrity, we took on this unique challenge and delivered lasting protection.

Schedule Free Inspection

BEFORE: Flooded Gravel Roof

The old tar and gravel roof had failed catastrophically, creating a waterlogged mess that resembled a small lake. Water infiltration was causing serious structural damage.

DURING: Precision Removal

Our team carefully removed the saturated materials while protecting the untouched half of the duplex. Proper containment and drainage were critical.

PROGRESS: TPO Installation

Professional installation of the TPO membrane system, ensuring perfect seams and proper drainage to prevent future water issues.

AFTER: Durable TPO System

The completed project shows a seamless, watertight TPO roof that will provide decades of reliable protection. Notice the clean transition between old and new sections.
